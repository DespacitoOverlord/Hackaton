Gaming Disorder

  • change in behavior pattern
  • leads to impairment in families and friends
  • It's defined in the 11th Revision of the International Classification of Diseases
  • Have trouble controlling the amount of time spent playing digital or video games
  • lacking control over their gaming habits
  • prioritizing gaming over other interests and activities
  • continuing gaming despite its negative consequences

These Problems affect..

  • Family life

  • Social Life

  • Personal Life

  • Education Life

  • Work Life
